Session Information

Book A Session Find A Session Near You

Can departments collaborate to reduce cost?

Absolutely! Several departments can collaborate, choose the sessions, and book a common room.  The good news is that cost is based on per-day basis and not per-participant. The list of the cities to be visited with dates and sponsors’ names will be promoted on GCconnex. This will facilitate opportunities for collaboration.

Where will the sessions take place?

A list of cities visited with dates and sponsors’ names will be promoted on GCconnex. This will facilitate opportunities for collaboration.

What if my city is not on the driving route?

If your city is not located on the Clicks and Tips Technology Tech Tour path, your office can still take part of this initiative using WebEx for a marginal fee. However, the sessions will be available to your city when Clicks and Tips is in the closest metropolitan area.

What is meant by “Crowdfunded” and what is expected?

As you may know, Clicks and Tips does not have any funding. The sponsoring department will pay for the day of work, prep time, one night hotel, meals, and other small charges. By going from city to city, the sponsoring department will not have to pay for the transportation or travel time making this initiative cheaper than hiring an outside firm or flying a speaker.

How many sessions do you deliver per day?

You can choose up to 3 credits per day. If you opt for more credits, please allow for an additional day.

In which languages do you deliver the sessions?

Either English or French.

How many requests can be accommodated per city?

Availability is limited to 1-2 days per city. Booking is on a "first come, first served" basis.

I am interested. What do I do next?

  1. Appropriate approval is required.
  2. Decide if you will be collaborating with others or keeping the sessions local.
  3. Select the sessions that you want.
  4. Email me the completed form including the financial cost center info.
  5. Book a suitable room for a large audience.
  6. To ensure the success of the sessions, assistance will be required. Please assign a person that can also assist with the Twitter promotion

How does this initiative align with the new Privy Council Clerk priorities?

  • Deliverology – Efficient delivery
  • Workplace health and wellness
